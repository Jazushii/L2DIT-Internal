What is Photography?

The word 'Photography' was derived from the greek words photo, which means 'light', and graph, which means to 'draw'. Together creating photography, which means 'drawing with light'. Drawing with light in a literal sense as it is the creation of images by recording light. Photography utilized in art, film, video production, magazines, or simply as a hobby.
