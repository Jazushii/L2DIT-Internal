Photography

What is Photography?

The word 'Photography' was derived from the greek words photo, which means 'light', and graph, which means to 'draw'. Together creating photography, which means 'drawing with light'. Drawing with light in a literal sense as it is the creation of images by recording light. Photography is utilized in art, film, video production, magazines, or simply as a hobby.

For more information, go to the Photography Wikipedia.

What does Level 2 Photography have to offer?

Karamu High School's Level 2 Photography course teaches us the various techniques in Photography. The general techniques utilized in photography are the rule of thirds, central composition, leading lines, negative space, form, and texture. These techniques can also be mixed and combined with each other to constuct a better photo.

We also get introduced the different photographty artists that all have different styles of photography. Each with their own story and concept behind every photo. Artists like Fiona Pardington, Christian Thompson, David Hilliard, Joachim Froese and much more.
